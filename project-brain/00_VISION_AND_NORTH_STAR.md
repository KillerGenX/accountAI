# Vision

## Build the World's First AI-Native Digital Employee Platform for Account Managers.

We believe Account Managers should spend their time building relationships, understanding customer needs, and winning opportunities—not searching for information, updating CRM records, preparing presentations, or writing reports.

Our mission is to create a future where every Account Manager works alongside a team of intelligent Digital Employees that operate 24 hours a day, continuously researching, analyzing, learning, and preparing actionable insights.

We are not building another CRM.

We are building an AI-native platform where humans and AI work together as one team.

---

# North Star

# Core Beliefs

## 🤖 AI Is a Digital Employee

AI is not a chatbot.

AI is not a prompt.

AI is not an assistant.

AI is a Digital Employee with responsibilities, objectives, memory, knowledge, and measurable performance.

---

## 👥 Humans Always Make the Final Decision

AI provides recommendations.

Humans make decisions.

The platform exists to augment human capability—not replace it.

---

## 📚 Every Recommendation Requires Evidence

Every AI-generated recommendation must include:

- Sources
- Confidence Score
- Timestamp
- Supporting Evidence
- Traceability

Trust is built through transparency.

---

## 🧠 AI Learns Continuously

Every feedback improves the platform.

Every successful opportunity becomes knowledge.

Every correction strengthens future recommendations.

Digital Employees never stop learning.

---

## ⚡ AI Works Before Humans

The best AI works before users ask.

Research should already be completed.

News should already be summarized.

Risks should already be identified.

Recommendations should already be prepared.

Users should start with answers—not empty screens.

---

## 🤝 AI Works as a Team

One AI can answer questions.

A team of AI can run a business.

Each Digital Employee has a dedicated role, responsibility, and expertise.

Examples include:

- Research Employee
- News Employee
- Industry Employee
- Opportunity Employee
- Relationship Employee
- Proposal Employee
- Forecast Employee
- Meeting Employee
- Renewal Employee

Together they form an intelligent digital workforce.

# Product Principles

## Account First

Everything starts with the Account.

Not Opportunities.

Not CRM records.

Not Reports.

The Account is the center of the platform.

---

## AI First

Whenever possible, AI completes the work before human involvement.

Humans validate.

AI prepares.

---

## Human Validation

Every AI-generated insight must be reviewable and verifiable by humans.

No hidden reasoning.

No black-box decisions.

---

## Continuous Learning

The platform becomes smarter every day through user feedback and historical outcomes.

---

## Modular by Design

Every new capability should be implemented as an independent module.

Never rebuild the foundation.

Always extend it.

---

## Event-Driven Architecture

Digital Employees communicate through events rather than direct dependencies.

Loose coupling enables unlimited scalability.

---

## AI Agnostic

The platform should never depend on a single AI provider.

Supported providers may include:

- OpenAI
- Google Gemini
- Anthropic Claude
- Open-source models

Technology evolves.

Architecture should remain stable.
